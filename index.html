FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about legal services, consultations, and the process.

How do I book an initial consultation with Advocate Javeria? +
You can book a consultation through our online appointment form, by calling our office, or via WhatsApp at +92 316 0990069. Initial consultations can be conducted in-person at our Peshawar office or via phone/video call for clients outside the city.
What documents should I bring to my first consultation? +
Bring any documents relevant to your case — court notices, contracts, property documents, NIC, marriage certificates (for family law), police FIR copies (for criminal matters), or any correspondence related to your legal issue. If unsure, bring everything and we will identify what is needed.
How are legal fees structured? +
Legal fees depend on the nature and complexity of your case. Advocate Javeria offers transparent, upfront fee structures discussed during the initial consultation. We offer various payment arrangements to ensure quality legal representation is accessible to everyone who needs it.
Do you handle cases outside of Peshawar? +
Yes. While our primary office is in Peshawar, Advocate Javeria practices in courts across KPK and can take cases in Islamabad High Court and Supreme Court of Pakistan. Remote consultations are also available for clients across Pakistan and internationally.
How long does a typical legal case take? +
Case timelines vary significantly based on case type, court schedule, and complexity. Simple matters may resolve in weeks; complex litigation can take months or years. Advocate Javeria always provides a realistic timeline assessment during consultation and keeps clients informed at every stage.
Is my case information kept confidential? +
Absolute confidentiality is the foundation of the attorney-client relationship. All information shared with Advocate Javeria is protected by strict professional privilege and will never be disclosed to any third party without your explicit consent.
