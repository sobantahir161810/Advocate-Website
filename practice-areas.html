Practice Area 01

Family Law & Domestic Matters

Family legal disputes are among the most emotionally charged situations a person can face. Advocate Javeria approaches each family matter with the sensitivity it deserves while maintaining the strategic rigor required to achieve the best possible legal outcome.

With extensive experience in Pakistan's family court system, she has successfully represented clients in hundreds of matters involving divorce, custody, inheritance, and domestic rights — always prioritizing the wellbeing of children and the fair treatment of all parties.

Services Include

  • Divorce proceedings (Khul'a and mutual divorce)
  • Child custody and visitation rights
  • Maintenance and alimony claims
  • Nikah nama review and enforcement
  • Inheritance and succession disputes
  • Domestic violence protection orders
  • Second marriage legal matters
  • Guardianship applications

Practice Area 02

Criminal Defense

When your liberty is at stake, you need an advocate who will fight without hesitation. Advocate Javeria provides robust criminal defense representation at every level — from initial FIR challenge to High Court bail hearings to trial acquittal.

She understands the human cost of criminal proceedings and works tirelessly to protect your constitutional rights, challenge procedural irregularities, and build the strongest possible defense based on facts, law, and forensic evidence.

Services Include

  • FIR quashing and challenge
  • Bail applications (pre-arrest & post-arrest)
  • Trial defense strategy and representation
  • Appeals against conviction
  • Constitutional petitions (Art. 199)
  • Fraud and financial crime defense
  • Cybercrime and PECA cases
  • Blasphemy and sensitive case defense
Consult on Criminal Defense

Practice Area 03

Civil Litigation

Civil disputes can consume years of your life and significant financial resources if not handled strategically from the outset. Advocate Javeria's civil litigation practice is defined by thorough case preparation, sharp procedural knowledge, and aggressive court advocacy.

Whether you are a plaintiff seeking justice or a defendant protecting your interests, her approach is always to assess the full legal landscape, explore settlement where appropriate, and fight decisively when litigation is necessary.

Services Include

  • Contract breach claims and defenses
  • Recovery suits and money claims
  • Defamation and reputation cases
  • Tort and negligence litigation
  • Injunctions and temporary orders
  • Civil appeals and revisions
  • Execution of decrees
  • Mediation and alternative dispute resolution
Consult on Civil Litigation
Practice Area 04

Property Disputes

Property disputes in Pakistan can be extraordinarily complex — involving competing title claims, fraudulent transfers, land grabbing, inheritance conflicts, and bureaucratic complications within the land registry system. Advocate Javeria has developed deep expertise in navigating all of these challenges.

She has successfully recovered unlawfully occupied properties, established title where documentation was incomplete or disputed, and protected clients against illegal encroachments through injunctions and criminal proceedings.

Services Include

  • Title disputes and ownership litigation
  • Land grabbing and illegal possession cases
  • Property transfer and inheritance claims
  • Tenancy disputes and eviction
  • Construction and boundary disputes
  • Fraudulent sale deed challenges
  • Revenue court matters (Patwari / Tehsildar)
  • Property registration and documentation
Consult on Property Law
