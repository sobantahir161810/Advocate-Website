Selected Case Outcomes

Diverse legal victories across all practice areas — from family courts to the Peshawar High Court.

✓ Successfully Resolved

Agricultural Land Ownership Dispute — 12 Acre Property

Court:Peshawar High Court
Area:Property Law
Duration:2 Years Litigation

Client's 12-acre agricultural land was fraudulently transferred by a relative using a forged sale deed. Advocate Javeria successfully challenged the transfer through forensic document analysis and witness testimony, securing full ownership restoration and additionally initiating criminal proceedings against the perpetrators under PPC 420 and 468.

✓ Acquittal Granted

False FIR Challenge — Criminal Defense, Section 302

Court:Sessions Court, Peshawar
Area:Criminal Defense
Duration:14 Months

Client faced false murder charges under Section 302 PPC filed by rival family. Advocate Javeria dismantled prosecution's case through cross-examination exposing contradictions, established a solid alibi with CCTV and witness evidence, resulting in full acquittal and the FIR being declared malicious.

✓ Full Custody Awarded

Child Custody — Two Minor Children, Contested Divorce

Court:Family Court, Peshawar
Area:Family Law
Duration:8 Months

Mother sought custody of two children aged 4 and 7 amid contested divorce proceedings. Advocate Javeria presented comprehensive welfare evidence, psychological assessments, and school records. Family court awarded full custody to the mother with structured visitation rights for the father.

✓ Contract Enforced + Damages

Commercial Contract Breach — Construction Company

Court:Civil Judge Court, Peshawar
Area:Corporate / Civil Law
Duration:11 Months

Client's construction company lost PKR 8 million due to a subcontractor's failure to deliver materials. Advocate Javeria filed a recovery suit, secured an interim injunction freezing the defendant's assets, and ultimately won full recovery of the principal amount plus 18% damages through a negotiated decree.

✓ Bail & Charges Dropped

PECA Cybercrime Case — Social Media Allegations

Court:Additional Sessions Court + PHC
Area:Criminal Defense
Duration:6 Months

Young professional faced PECA charges for alleged defamatory social media posts he did not make. Advocate Javeria secured urgent bail from Peshawar High Court within 72 hours, then challenged FIR on constitutional grounds under Articles 19 and 25. Charges were withdrawn after digital forensic evidence proved client's innocence.

✓ Inheritance Rights Secured

Women's Inheritance Dispute — Ancestral Property

Court:Civil Court + PHC Appeal
Area:Family / Property Law
Duration:3 Years

Three sisters were denied their rightful share of their father's ancestral property by male relatives. Advocate Javeria pursued the matter through civil court and a subsequent PHC appeal, arguing both Pakistani inheritance law and Islamic law entitlements. The sisters received their full legal share of the property valued at PKR 45 million.

✓ Divorce & Maintenance Decree

Khul'a Proceedings — Domestic Abuse Survivor

Court:Family Court, Mardan
Area:Family Law
Duration:5 Months

Domestic abuse survivor sought Khul'a divorce and protection from her abusive spouse. Advocate Javeria expedited proceedings, secured a protection order within days, and obtained a divorce decree along with a monthly maintenance order of PKR 35,000 for the client and her children's financial security.

✓ Illegal Possession Removed

Urban Property — Illegal Occupancy & Encroachment

Court:Civil Court, Nowshera
Area:Property Law
Duration:7 Months

Client's residential plot in Nowshera was illegally occupied by neighbors who had encroached on 6 marlas of land over two decades. Advocate Javeria secured an urgent injunction, presented historical ownership records and survey maps, and obtained a possession decree restoring the full plot area to the rightful owner.
