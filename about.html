Biography

Advocate Javeria —
Defender of Rights

"The law is not merely a profession to me — it is my calling, my responsibility, and my promise to every client who walks through my door."

Advocate Javeria is a distinguished legal professional based in Peshawar, Pakistan, with over 12 years of dedicated practice spanning some of the country's most complex and sensitive legal matters. She obtained her LLB from the University of Peshawar and her LLM in Constitutional Law from Quaid-i-Azam University, Islamabad.

Enrolled as an advocate of the Peshawar High Court, she has appeared in district courts, sessions courts, the Peshawar High Court, and the Supreme Court of Pakistan. Her practice is distinguished by meticulous preparation, sharp courtroom instincts, and a genuine commitment to her clients' wellbeing.

As a female advocate in a field historically dominated by men, Advocate Javeria has broken barriers and become a trusted voice for women, families, and individuals seeking justice across Khyber Pakhtunkhwa and beyond. She is a member of the Peshawar Bar Association and the KPK Women Lawyers Forum.

Career Journey

Professional Timeline

A journey of growth, learning, and an unwavering commitment to legal excellence.

2011
LLB Graduation
Graduated with distinction from the Faculty of Law, University of Peshawar, with a focus on Constitutional and Family Law.
2012
Bar Enrollment
Enrolled as an advocate with the Peshawar Bar Association. Began practice at district courts under Senior Advocate Habib-ur-Rehman.
2014
LLM — Constitutional Law
Completed Master of Laws with specialization in Constitutional Law from Quaid-i-Azam University, Islamabad, enhancing expertise in fundamental rights litigation.
2015
High Court Enrollment
Enrolled as an advocate of the Peshawar High Court. Began taking complex property, criminal, and family law matters at the High Court level.
2017
Founded Independent Practice
Established Advocate Javeria Law Chambers in Peshawar — a boutique practice delivering premium, personalized legal services across KPK.
2019
KPK Women Lawyers Forum
Appointed Vice-Chair of the KPK Women Lawyers Forum, advocating for gender equality in the legal profession and access to justice for women.
2021
Supreme Court Practice
Gained Supreme Court of Pakistan enrollment, enabling constitutional petitions and appeals at the nation's highest judicial authority.
2025
Today — Continued Excellence
Continues delivering premium legal counsel with 500+ cases handled, 98% success rate, and an expanding practice serving clients across Pakistan.
